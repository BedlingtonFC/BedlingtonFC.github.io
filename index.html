Welcome to Bedlington FC

Formed in 2017 following the succesful merger of Bedlington Town FC,
Bedlington Juniors and Hazelmere United, Bedlington FC offers opportunities for all to participate in sport, where inclusion is at the heart of what we represent and where boys, girls, men and women
play under the same ethos. It is where friendships are created between players and parents alike,
in a safe and enjoyable environment.

FOUNDATION YEARS

Reception and Year One children can start their football journey, building a love of the game in our weekly mixed sessions with our FA Qualified Coaching Team

Local club

We give children from the area an opportunity to enjoy being part of a football club. We'll develop their technique, skills and fitness in a safe and positive environment. Football is the centre of our activities, being involved in a club helps teach youngsters many social skills including teamwork, reliability, friendship and loyalty

Social Skills

Having a social environment for the children and parents is high on our club agenda, we want you to feel part of something bigger than any one team to help you and your family get the most out of grass roots football. We organise events away from football throughout the year, and you should never miss our legendary end of season presentation!
